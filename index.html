Drilling Al‑6061 Optimization

Multi‑response optimization of feed/speed/diameter for hole quality; SEM‑backed analysis.

Objective

Optimize drilling parameters to improve hole quality in Al-6061 alloy for aerospace applications.

Methods

Employed Design of Experiments (DOE), ANOVA, and MATLAB for multi-response analysis; validated with SEM imaging.

Findings & Impact

Geopolymer‑Stabilized Soil

GBFS+RHA mix achieving 4.33 MPa UCS at 28‑days under sulfate exposure.

Objective

Enhance soil stability using sustainable geopolymer materials for construction in sulfate-rich environments.

Methods

Mixed Ground Blast Furnace Slag (GBFS) and Rice Husk Ash (RHA); conducted UCS testing and SEM/EDS analysis.

Findings & Impact

Achieved 4.33 MPa compressive strength; promotes eco-friendly alternatives to cement, reducing environmental impact.
